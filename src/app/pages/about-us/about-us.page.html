About Us
This app aims to help local sugarcane farmers and researchers in classifying sugarcane varieties available in farm fields. This application could only recognize 5 sugarcane varieties so far. There are two ways to select images for the model to classify: (1) Select cropped sugarcane stalk image from local storage, (2) Capture image using back camera and crop the region of interest intended for usage.
